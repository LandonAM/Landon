Collaborative Study of Dungeons & Dragons.

Academic Researcher

In the Collaborative Study of Dungeons & Dragons me and my team worked together to analyze each individual edition of D&D over the years and breakdown their pros and cons in order to accurately assess why the 5th edition printing of D&D was the most popular with a large social resurgence. For my part I assisted in analyzing an early predecessor to D&D from Gary Gygax titled “Chainmail”. We participated in a few different experiments and assessed the rules of the games both in theory and in practice.

This is our final Collaborative Analysis and paper with our findings and assessment.
